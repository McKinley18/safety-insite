# Sentinel Safety Capability Index

This index tracks the major functional capabilities of the Sentinel Safety platform, focusing on AI safety, regulatory logic, and field inspection workflows.

## SafeScope Core Reasoning
- **Hazard Taxonomy Routing:** Weighted signal matching for 40+ domains.
- **Approved Knowledge Retrieval:** Deterministic matching against source-backed registries.
- **Field Evidence Weighting:** Contradiction detection and confidence scoring.
- **Multi-Hazard Decomposition:** Phrase splitting for complex observations.
- **Cross-Domain Causal Chains:** Compound risk interaction reasoning.
- **Corrective Action Ranking:** Deterministic prioritization of controls.
- **Risk Verification:** Residual risk reassessment and action quality analysis.

## Governance & Explainability
- **Source Freshness Governance:** Regulation currency monitor.
- **Jurisdiction Decision Tree:** Defensive regulatory routing.
- **Explainability:** Audit-ready reasoning traces with structured decision paths.
- **Human-in-the-Loop Learning:** Governed feedback loops for expert corrections.
- **Knowledge Source Ingestion:** Controlled growth pipeline for new sources.
- **Reviewer Candidate Console:** Centralized management for staged improvements.

## Specialized Intelligence
- **Semantic Synonym Expansion:** Recognizes equivalent safety language across domains.
- **Visual Evidence Reasoning:** Metadata-based analysis of inspection photos and notes.
- **Visual Evidence API + Inspection UI Wiring:** Full integration of photo metadata into reasoning and field assessments.
