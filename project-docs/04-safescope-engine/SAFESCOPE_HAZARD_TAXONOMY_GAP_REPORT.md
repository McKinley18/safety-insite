## Summary of Findings
- **Covered Domains:** 8 core domains are fully covered by draft packs with validation.
- **Routing-Only Domains:** 9 domains (including forklifts, conveyors) are currently supported by routing logic but lack dedicated draft packs.
- **Priority Gaps:** High/Critical gaps include fire protection, rigging/lifting, hot work, and cranes/hoists.
- **Action Plan:** Prioritize development of draft packs for "critical" gap domains.
