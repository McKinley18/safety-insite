# SafeScope Full Hazard Coverage Expansion Map v1

This document outlines the expanded hazard universe coverage for SafeScope AI Intelligence, ensuring broad reasoning capabilities across acute safety and chronic health hazards.

## Expanded Hazard Families

### Acute Safety Hazards
- **Machine Guarding:** Rotating parts, nip points, interlocks.
- **Lockout/Tagout (LOTO):** Unexpected startup, stored energy isolation.
- **Electrical Safety:** Energized parts, arc flash, grounding.
- **Fall Protection:** Open edges, floor holes, elevated work.
- **Mobile Equipment:** Powered industrial trucks, pedestrian segregation.
- **Powered Haulage:** Conveyors, surface haulage.
- **Material Handling:** Unstable stacks, industrial racking.
- **Cranes & Rigging:** Suspended loads, rigging defects.
- **Excavation & Trenching:** Cave-in protection, shoring.
- **Fire & Explosion:** Flammable storage, suppression.
- **Hot Work:** Welding, cutting, fire watch.
- **Combustible Dust:** Accumulation control, explosion venting.
- **Pressure Systems:** Hydraulic, pneumatic, pressure vessels.
- **Chemical Transfer:** Bonding, grounding, secondary containment.

### Chronic Health Hazards
- **Silica Dust:** Dry cutting suppression, HEPA collection.
- **Welding Fumes:** Local exhaust ventilation, base metal toxicosis.
- **Occupational Noise:** Acoustic enclosure, hearing conservation.
- **Heat Stress:** WBGT-based rest/water/shade.
- **Cold Stress:** Thermal protection, acclimation.
- **Chemical Inhalation:** Toxic vapor control, PEL monitoring.
- **Respiratory Protection:** Fit testing, cartridge selection.
- **Ergonomics:** Mechanical assists, biomechanical strain.
- **Biological Hazards:** Bloodborne pathogens, sanitation.

### Emergency Readiness & Mixed
- **Emergency Egress:** Exit paths, illumination.
- **Emergency Equipment:** Showers, eyewashes, extinguishers.
- **Air Quality:** Ventilation turnover, CO2 monitoring.

## Generalization vs. Memorization
SafeScope avoids scenario memorization by:
1.  **Energy-Agent Logic:** Identifying the underlying physics of harm.
2.  **Hierarchy of Controls:** Prioritizing engineering/isolation over administrative fixes.
3.  **Anti-Regurgitation:** Explicitly blocking "retrain only" as a primary fix for high-energy hazards.
