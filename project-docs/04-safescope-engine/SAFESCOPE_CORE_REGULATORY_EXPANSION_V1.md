# SafeScope Core Regulatory Expansion Pack v1

This document outlines the expansion of SafeScope’s core regulatory knowledge base, specifically targeting identified coverage gaps in OSHA and MSHA standards.

## Purpose
The Regulatory Coverage Matrix (v1) identified 20 missing or incomplete core regulatory standards. This expansion pack bridges these gaps by introducing source-backed, normalized knowledge records into the SafeScope knowledge staging pipeline.

## Core Expansion Areas
- **OSHA General Industry:** Targets gaps in 1910 Subpart D (Walking-Working Surfaces).
- **MSHA Metal/Nonmetal:** Targets gaps in 30 CFR 56/57 Subpart I (Traffic Control and Mobile Equipment).

## Governance Boundaries
- **Reviewer-Gated Promotion:** All new records are introduced as `draft_candidate` records. They **do not** immediately enter the approved registry.
- **Role-Based Approval Gates:** Records are subject to review by authorized roles (e.g., OSHA GI Reviewer, MSHA MNM Reviewer) using the existing approval workflow.
- **Normalization:** Each record adheres to the established normalization and deduplication standards, including hazard family mapping and citation normalization.
- **Advisory-Only:** All content remains `advisoryOnly: true`.

## Future Expansion
This pack serves as the first iteration of core regulatory expansion. Future packs will address the remaining 18 identified missing core standards, focusing on high-risk domains like confined space, lockout/tagout, and construction-specific excavation hazards.
